About

The Blog

A data centric blog helping to fulfill the motto Learn, Explore, Share.

This blog will host various posts related to data science and focus on Data-Preparation, Data Visualization, Deep Dives, Quick Starts, How To's, and other Miscellaneous topics of interest.

If you enjoy what you read and find it helpful please check back, and check back often, click here and follow me on Medium, and subscribe to the Inquisitive Nature publication.

Who I am

I am a naturally inquisitive person who finds himself in a constant state of curiosity. I feed this curiosity by learning and exploring data tools and techniques with a special interest in data preparation and visualization. The creativity, collaboration, and problem solving required to visualize and derive significant insights or discovery from raw messy data is what keeps me driven.

I thrive on utilizing data analytics, creative thinking, and effective design to distill and communicate the key insights of a dataset. These insights are the fuel for data-driven solutions.

Do you have insights locked away in data? Lets unlock them!
